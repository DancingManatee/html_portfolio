E's Movies and Memories

Movies that have impacted me and take me back
... in no particular order

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles

I still remember watching this movie with my brothers in the living room of one of our old houses. I can recite more lines from this movie than any other. Like many other movies it inspired us to fight with eachother. KOWABUNGA!!!

Bloodsport

Another movie that inspired us to fight. So much ass kicking in this one. The scene where Chun-li pops a mans shin bone out of his leg still gives me the willies. "Now I break you, like I break your friend!"

Home Alone

This one is a change of pace from the others. We loved watching this movie as kids and imagined all kinds of booby traps, should we need to defend our home from burglers. I am fortunate that I get to continue watching this movie with my family ever Christmas.
